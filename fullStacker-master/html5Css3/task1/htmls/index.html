The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neigborhoods an enviroment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member f NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer tim of parents and previous league members.
